Site Plan:

Point of the Mountain PT

This site name represents a physical therapy clinic that is located in Utah at the point of the mountain.

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to connect the community to Physical Therapy. This site will be covering what services PT offers/what to expect from a visit, What job's are available through the company, and how to Join the team or make an appointment.

Questions or Scenarios

- What is PT?

- What PT do I need?

- How do I sign up for PT

- What do careers look like for a PT related field?

- why would I want to work as a physical therapist?

- what can PT do for me?

Colors

I plan to use Navy and rgb(228, 209, 175) or "tan" I plan to use the tan for the background and the navy for the outline of the header tags I also plan to use the navy as the font color.

Typography

I plan to use Georgia as a the font, and I am going to use it for everything on this document. all header tags, p elements, alt tags ect...
